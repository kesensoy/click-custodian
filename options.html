Tab Auto-Close Rules

When a tab's URL matches one of these patterns, Click Custodian shows a countdown overlay and closes the tab. Rules sync across your browsers — use Import and Export to share or back them up.

Button Auto-Click Rules

Match a URL, resolve a CSS selector (optionally filtered by text), wait the configured delay, then click. Polls for up to 3000ms with a MutationObserver before giving up.
